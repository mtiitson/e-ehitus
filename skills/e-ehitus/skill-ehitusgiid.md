# Ehitusgiid — Practical Rules Reference

Source: https://livekluster.ehr.ee/ui/ehr/v1/abi/ehitusgiid (scraped 2026-04-24)

**Note:** The Ehitusgiid itself warns it does NOT yet include ehitusseadustik changes effective 28.03.2026. Use Lisa 1 for authoritative permit thresholds.

## Activity type definitions

Understanding which activity type applies determines the document type and required fields.

### Püstitamine (new construction)
Building a new structure, including:
- Building on an old foundation
- Modular house construction
- Disassembling an existing building and reassembling elsewhere
- Temporary building construction

### Ümberehitamine (renovation/reconstruction)
Changing building properties or materials. Includes:
- Insulation (interior or exterior)
- Roof structure/covering changes
- Building out a floor without raising the ridge
- New door/window openings or closing existing ones
- Window replacement when material, size, or placement changes
- Load-bearing or bracing wall changes
- Foundation changes (including insulation)
- Restoring a partially or fully destroyed building
- Demolition combined with reconstruction
- Technical systems (heating, water, electrical, ventilation) installation/modification when it significantly changes building properties or facade (e.g. heat pump on facade)

**NOT ümberehitamine:**
- Replacing individual parts with equivalent ones = **remont**
- Minor interior changes (moving pipes for kitchen relocation, adding lightweight partitions) = **remont**
- If building volume changes (height and/or footprint) = **laiendamine**

### Laiendamine (extension)
Adding to an existing building. Building volume and area change:
- Balcony, veranda, windbreak, terrace construction
- Raising the roof

### Lammutamine (demolition)
Removing or liquidating a building without further construction activity.

**NOT lammutamine:**
- Partial demolition followed by extension beyond original volume = **laiendamine**
- Replacing demolished building with similar one (same purpose, architecture, volume) = **ümberehitamine** (reconstruction)
- Building a fundamentally new building on the same site/foundation = **püstitamine**

### Remont (repair)
Finishing work or replacing parts with equivalents. Volume and area do not change:
- Painting, plastering repairs, interior finishing
- Replacing windows with equivalent ones
- Replacing facade material with equivalent
- Demolishing non-load-bearing partition walls

## Permit thresholds (hoone — complements Lisa 1)

### Elamu
- **≤20 m² and ≤5 m**: no permit/notice needed
- **20–60 m², ≤5 m**: ehitusteatis (+ ehitusprojekt for püstitamine/lammutamine)
- **>60 m² or >5 m**: ehitusluba for püstitamine/lammutamine; ehitusteatis + ehitusprojekt for ümberehitamine

### Key rule
Even when no permit is required, construction must still comply with: planning documents, safety/environmental requirements, restrictions (e.g. heritage), and must not violate neighbors' rights.

### Ümberehitamine is always teavituskohustuslik
Renovation always requires at minimum an ehitusteatis. For buildings >5 m tall or >60 m² footprint, ehitusprojekt is also required.

## Building measurement rules

### Ehitisealune pind (building footprint)
**Includes:** ground projection of above-ground and underground parts, balconies, loggias, cellars, permanent canopies >2 m².
**Excludes:** rainwater system, ramp, stairs, sun shading, technical systems/equipment, terrace, roof eaves ≤1 m wide.

### Kõrgus (height)
Measured from average surrounding ground level to highest structural point. Average ground level = (max + min height of building perimeter) / 2.

Maximum height may be set by detail/general plan or design conditions (projekteerimistingimused).

## Living space requirements (eluruumid)

Does NOT apply to suvila (summer house) or aiamaja (garden house).

- Each living/work/bedroom: **min 8 m²** area, **min 2.4 m** width
- Ceiling height: **min 2.5 m** (or **2.3 m** in single-unit residential building)

## Energy certificate requirements

- New buildings must meet energy efficiency minimum requirements for ehitusluba
- **Since 2020:** new buildings require **A-class** energy efficiency (liginullenergiahoone)
- **Exception:** buildings ≤220 m² heated area may meet **B-class** (madalaenergia­hoone)
- Energy certificate (energiamärgis) shows ETA value and energy class (A=best, H=worst)

## Fire safety

- Fire safety gap (tuleohutuskuja) between buildings: **min 8 m** (measured from protruding parts)
- Usually split equally: **4 m from each property boundary**
- Building closer affects fire safety requirements for future neighboring construction

## Planning and zoning

- **Üldplaneering** (general plan): sets land use, building parameters, infrastructure
- **Detailplaneering** (detailed plan): required in dense areas, sets exact building placement, height, volume
- **Projekteerimistingimused** (design conditions): when no detailed plan exists, issued by local government. Valid **5 years**.

## Heritage and nature protection

- **Muinsuskaitsealal** (heritage area): new construction only with detailed plan heritage special conditions (eritingimused). May set height limits, volumes, architectural style.
- **Ehituskeeluvöönd** (construction exclusion zone): near water bodies, generally no new construction allowed
- Check with Keskkonnaamet (Environmental Board) for nature protection restrictions

## Technical systems

### Heating (küte)
- Heat pump on facade (e.g. õhksoojuspump) = ümberehitamine (changes facade)
- **Kaugküttepiirkond** (district heating zone): local government may require connection to district heating network when building new or renovating
- Check general plan for district heating zones

### Ventilation
- **Loomulik ventilatsioon**: via windows and passive openings
- **Mehaaniline/sundventilatsioon**: powered, usually automatic. Subtypes:
  - Exhaust only (väljatõmme)
  - Supply + exhaust (sissepuhe ja väljatõmme)
  - With heat recovery (soojustagastusega)

### Water supply and sewerage
- **Ühisveevärk** (public water supply): connection via vee-ettevõtja (water company)
- **Puurkaev** (drilled well): requires hüdrogeoloogiliste tööde tegevusluba (geological works permit), must register in EELIS
- **Salvkaev** (dug well): no geological permit needed, min distance from pollution sources
- **Reoveemahuti** (septic tank): for areas without public sewerage
- **Reoveepuhasti** (treatment plant): local wastewater treatment

### Electrical systems
- Grid connection (võrk) = most common
- Local production: solar (päikesepaneel), wind, cogeneration
- Electrical installations must be documented and inspected

## Neighbor rights

Neighbors must be consulted when construction may affect their rights. Specifically prohibited:
- Excavating or raising ground level at property boundary endangering neighbor's buildings
- Directing rainwater onto neighbor's property
- Building an accessory building at property boundary with roof slope toward neighbor (unless they agree)

## Self-building (ise ehitamine)

The owner may build their own house, but must:
- Comply with construction project
- Meet all building and usage requirements (safety, environmental fit, etc.)
- Obtain permits/notices
- Submit construction project
- Order expert review if needed
- Document the construction process
